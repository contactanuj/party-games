## Word-deduction games (Imposter, Out of the Loop, Spy Hunt)

These implement well-known *folk* social-deduction formats (a hidden "odd one out" found through
clues, questions, or questioning). The mechanics are not protected by copyright, and all word lists,
question banks, location/role sets, copy, themes, and icons here are written from scratch.

- **Spy Hunt** uses the location-and-roles questioning format popularly associated with the
  commercial game *Spyfall*. "Spyfall" is a trademark of its publisher; this project is **not**
  affiliated with or endorsed by it, uses a **different name** and **original** locations/roles, and
  copies no rulebook text or artwork.
- **Out of the Loop** and **Imposter** are generic secret-word formats with original content.

If you fork these apps, keep the names generic and the content original.
